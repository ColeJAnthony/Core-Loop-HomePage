We game because we love the play: in play we encounter joy, challenge, and wonder. We forge friendships and shape memories, while catching glimpses of ourselves and the world in new ways. Games, when done right, open up spaces where community and meaning take root.

Yet games are more than play. They are the intersection of art, engineering, and the latest technology — a medium where music, design, storytelling, and engineering intertwine, creating experiences unlike anything else.

And games are more than a medium. As an over $180 billion dollar a year industry, it helps power economies, creates careers, and provides countless people the chance to pursue a career in their passion.

Core Loop exists to keep people plugged in to every part of this ecosystem: reviews and analysis that illuminate the art, guides and walkthroughs for our perfectionist/ completionist, and education and insight for those who want to step into the industry themselves.

In time, Core Loop intends to become part of the connective tissue of this living, evolving industry — carrying information, games, news, and people across its networks to push the medium forward and, in the process, help it change more lives.
